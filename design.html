Fat Stacks Brainstorming

Fat stacks teaches people about finances and how to acquire assets to get rich.

Rules

  • you have a countdown timer set to five years
  • each event or major purchase takes time away from the countdown
  • you win the game by getting enough cashflow to cover your expenses plus a million dollars net worth
  • if you go bankrupt you lose
